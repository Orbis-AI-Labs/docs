## Get started in 4 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.meetorbis.com](https://app.meetorbis.com) with your email or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your contacts">
    Upload a CSV or connect your LinkedIn archive to seed your network instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Google">
    Sync Gmail and Google Calendar to automatically track interactions with your contacts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start exploring">
    Use the dashboard to see your relationship health, or try Deep Search to find anyone in your network.
  </Step>
</Steps>
