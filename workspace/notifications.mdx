title: "Notifications"
description: "Stay informed about comments, mentions, task updates, and team activity in your Orbis workspace."
---

Notifications keep you up to date on activity across your workspace — comments, @mentions, task assignments, due dates, and more.

## Viewing notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top-right corner of the app to open the notifications popover. Notifications appear in a feed with the most recent at the top.

Each notification includes:

- Who triggered it (e.g., a teammate who mentioned you)
- What happened (e.g., "mentioned you in a comment on Jane Smith")
- A direct link to the relevant record

Click any notification to navigate directly to the record it references.

## What triggers notifications

| Event | Notification sent to |
|-------|---------------------|
| **@mention in a comment** | The mentioned team member |
| **Comment on a record you're following** | All followers of that record |
| **Task assigned to you** | The assignee |
| **Task due date reached** | The assignee |
| **Agent requires approval** | Board members / admins |
| **Invitation to organization** | The invitee (via email) |

## Real-time updates

Notifications are delivered in real-time — you don't need to refresh the page. When a new notification arrives, the bell icon shows an unread indicator.

## Managing notifications

- **Mark as read** — notifications are marked as read when you click them
- **Notification preferences** — configure which notifications you receive in **Settings → Notifications**

<Tip>
  If you're getting too many notifications, adjust your preferences in Settings. You can turn off notifications for specific event types while keeping the ones that matter.
</Tip>
