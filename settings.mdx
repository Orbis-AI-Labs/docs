Access Settings by clicking your avatar in the sidebar and choosing **Settings**, or by pressing `Cmd+,` (`Ctrl+,` on Windows/Linux).

## Profile

The **Profile** section lets you update your personal and company information. Orbis uses this context to personalize AI drafts and enrich your data.

### Personal information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **First name / Last name** | Your display name throughout Orbis |
| **Email** | Your primary login email (managed through your Google account) |
| **Personal email** | An alternate email used for profile enrichment — add a personal Gmail if your primary address is a work email |
| **Bio** | A short description about yourself |
| **URLs** | Links to your website, LinkedIn, or social profiles |
| **Language** | Your preferred language for the Orbis interface |

### Company information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Company domain** | Your company's website URL (e.g., `https://acme.com`) |
| **Your role** | Your job function (Founder, Sales, Engineering, etc.) |
| **Company description** | What your company does and what you want to accomplish with Orbis |

Orbis uses the company description to write better AI-drafted emails on your behalf.

### Security

Orbis accounts are secured through Google. To manage passwords, two-factor authentication, and security keys, visit your [Google Account security settings](https://myaccount.google.com/security).

## Notifications

The **Notifications** section controls when and how Orbis alerts you.

### In-app notifications

Choose which activity triggers in-app notifications:

- **All new messages** — notify for every new message
- **Direct messages and mentions** — notify only when you're mentioned or messaged directly
- **Nothing** — disable in-app notifications

### Email notifications

| Toggle | Controls |
|--------|---------|
| **Communication emails** | Emails about your account activity |
| **Marketing emails** | Product updates, new features, and announcements |
| **Social emails** | Follows, connection requests, and similar activity |
| **Security emails** | Account security alerts (always on, cannot be disabled) |

Click **Update notifications** to save your changes.

## Connected accounts

The **Connected Accounts** section shows which third-party accounts are linked to Orbis.

Orbis currently supports:

- **Google** — sync Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Contacts
- **LinkedIn** — import your connections archive

Managing a connected account (reconnecting, revoking access, or checking sync status) is done from the **Integrations** section. See [Google integration](/integrations/google) and [LinkedIn integration](/integrations/linkedin) for step-by-step instructions.

## Appearance

### Theme

Orbis supports light mode, dark mode, and system-default mode. Switch themes from the appearance settings, or cycle through all three with the keyboard shortcut `Cmd+Shift+L` (`Ctrl+Shift+L` on Windows/Linux).

### Language

Select your preferred language from the dropdown. Orbis supports right-to-left (RTL) layouts for Arabic, Hebrew, and other RTL languages — the entire interface reflows automatically when an RTL language is selected.

## Billing and subscription

The **Billing** section shows your current plan and available features.

### Free plan

The free plan includes:
- Up to 500 contacts
- 1 connected email account
- Basic search and filtering
- Email sync
- Calendar sync

### Pro plan

The Pro plan adds:
- Unlimited contacts
- Up to 5 connected email accounts
- Team members and collaboration
- Advanced search and filtering
- Advanced integrations
- Activity audit logs
- Priority support

Click **Upgrade to Pro** to start a Pro subscription. Billing history and invoices appear in this section once you're on the Pro plan.

<Note>
  Pro plan subscriptions are coming soon. Click **Upgrade to Pro** to be notified when billing is available.
</Note>

## Data & privacy

### Export your data

To export all of your data from Orbis, contact support. A full data export includes your contacts, notes, campaigns, custom object records, and settings.

### Delete your account

To permanently delete your account, scroll to the **Danger Zone** section on the Profile settings page and click **Delete Account**.

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your contacts, notes, campaigns, boards, custom objects, and settings will be deleted immediately.
</Warning>

You'll be asked to confirm before deletion proceeds. After your account is deleted, any connected Google and LinkedIn integrations are automatically disconnected.
