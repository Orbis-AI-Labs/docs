## What it checks

- **SPF** — is Google authorized to send on behalf of your domain?
- **DKIM** — is a digital signature configured for your emails?
- **DMARC** — do you have a policy for handling unauthenticated emails?
- **MX Records** — are your mail exchange records properly configured?

Each failed check includes step-by-step instructions to fix the issue.
