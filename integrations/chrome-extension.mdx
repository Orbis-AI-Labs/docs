## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit any website">
    Navigate to a company website like stripe.com, linear.app, or any other domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Orbis icon">
    Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar. Orbis automatically detects the page type and shows the right view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See intelligence instantly">
    Company sites show full company profiles. Person profile pages show enriched person data. Event and social pages offer contact extraction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Smart detection

The extension automatically detects what you're looking at:

| Page type | What Orbis shows |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Any company website** | Company profile — funding, people, financials, hiring, news, social profiles |
| **Person profiles** | Enriched person data — work history, education, social links, article mentions |
| **Luma events** | Contact extraction — extract event attendees with social links |
| **Instagram** | Contact extraction — extract followers and following lists |

## What data you get

### Company intelligence

- Funding rounds, total raised, valuation, and investors
- Key people with titles and LinkedIn profiles
- SEC financials — revenue, net income, market cap (public companies)
- Hiring data — open roles by department with direct links
- Recent news and press coverage
- Social profiles — LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, Crunchbase
- Traction metrics, customer relationships, and ProductHunt launches

### Person profiles

- Current role, company, and work history
- Education background
- Social links — LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub
- Article mentions and publications

## Installing the extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Chrome Web Store">
    Go to the [Orbis Chrome Extension](https://meetorbis.com/extension) page and click **Add to Chrome**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pin the extension">
    Click the puzzle piece icon in your browser toolbar and pin Orbis for easy access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Click the Orbis icon and sign in with your Google account. This connects the extension to your Orbis workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The extension works without signing in — you'll see a preview of company data. Sign in to unlock full intelligence and contact import.
</Note>
