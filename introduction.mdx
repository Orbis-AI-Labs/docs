Orbis brings your contacts, communications, and interactions into one intelligent workspace. It connects to Gmail and Google Calendar, learns from your network activity, and uses AI to surface the right information at the right time — so you spend less time managing data and more time building relationships.

## Who Orbis is for

Orbis is built for founders, sales professionals, recruiters, investors, and anyone else whose success depends on their relationships. If you maintain a professional network and want a smarter way to track interactions, follow up consistently, and stay on top of your contacts, Orbis is for you.

## How Orbis is different

Most CRMs are designed to store data. Orbis is designed to help you act on it.

**AI-first, not AI-bolted-on.** AI is built into the core of Orbis — from how contacts are enriched automatically to how Deep Search understands the meaning behind your questions, not just keywords.

**Relationship intelligence, not just a contact list.** Orbis tracks interaction frequency, sentiment, and recency across email and calendar to give you a real picture of each relationship. You'll know who you haven't spoken to in months, who introduced you to whom, and what topics you've discussed with any contact.

**Gmail and Calendar as first-class integrations.** Rather than asking you to manually log every meeting and email, Orbis syncs your Google data and does the work for you. Your interaction history builds itself.
