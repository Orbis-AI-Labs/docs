AI Drafts lets Orbis write email replies and outreach messages for you. Each draft is personalized using your full relationship history — past emails, calendar events, notes, and enrichment data for that contact.

## How AI drafts work

When you request a draft, Orbis:

1. Reads the conversation thread (for replies) or the contact's full profile (for new outreach)
2. Pulls in relationship context — email history, calendar events, notes, interaction signals
3. Generates a personalized draft using AI
4. Saves it for your review before sending

You always review and edit before sending — AI drafts are never sent automatically.

## Generating a reply draft

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a thread">
    Select an email thread in the Inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a draft">
    Click **Generate reply** in the thread toolbar or row action menu. Orbis reads the conversation and writes a contextual reply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit">
    The compose window opens with the AI-generated draft. Edit as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **Send** when you're ready. The reply is sent via your connected Gmail account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generating drafts for contacts

You can generate outreach drafts for individual contacts or in batch:

### Single contact

From a contact's profile, click **Generate AI Draft**. Choose a goal template:

| Template | Use case |
|----------|----------|
| **Outreach** | First-time introduction or cold outreach |
| **Ask Question** | Request information or advice |
| **Give Feedback** | Share feedback or a recommendation |
| **Re-engage** | Reconnect after a period of silence |

### Batch drafts

Select up to 20 contacts in the contacts list, then choose **Generate Drafts** from the bulk actions toolbar. Each contact gets a personalized draft based on their individual profile and your relationship history.

## The Generated Drafts folder

All AI-generated drafts are saved to the **Generated Drafts** folder in your Inbox sidebar. From here you can:

- Review each draft before sending
- Edit the AI-generated content
- Send individual drafts or use **Send All Drafts**
- Discard drafts you don't want to send

## Draft statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Generated and ready for review |
| **Sending** | Currently being sent |
| **Sent** | Delivered successfully |
| **Failed** | Delivery failed — check the error and retry |
| **Discarded** | You chose not to send this draft |

## Drafts in campaigns

When you add contacts to a campaign, Orbis automatically generates a draft for each contact based on the campaign's goal. These drafts appear in the campaign's contact table and follow the same review-before-send flow.

See [Campaigns](/campaigns) for details on campaign-specific draft generation.

<Tip>
  AI drafts preserve both the original generated text and your edits separately. If you edit a draft and want to start over, you can revert to the AI-generated version.
</Tip>
